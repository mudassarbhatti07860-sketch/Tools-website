💡 Quick SEO Tips
Tip 01
Target Long-Tail Keywords
Long-tail keywords (3+ words) have lower competition and higher conversion rates. Use Keyword Research tool to find them.
Tip 02
Optimize Title Tags
Keep titles between 50–60 characters. Include your primary keyword near the beginning for maximum SEO impact.
Tip 03
Write Compelling Meta Descriptions
Meta descriptions don't affect rankings directly but boost click-through rates. Aim for 140–160 characters with a call to action.
Tip 04
Fix Broken Links
Broken links damage user experience and waste crawl budget. Use Broken Link Checker regularly to keep your site healthy.
Tip 05
Add Schema Markup
Structured data (Schema) helps search engines understand your content and enables rich snippets in search results.
Tip 06
Improve Page Speed
Google uses page speed as a ranking factor. Aim for under 3 seconds. Use Page Speed Checker to identify issues.

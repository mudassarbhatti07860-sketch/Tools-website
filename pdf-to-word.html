❓ Frequently Asked Questions – PDF to Word Converter

Is this PDF to Word converter free and safe?
Yes, completely free and 100% safe. This converter works entirely in your browser using PDF.js — your PDF file is never uploaded to any server, never stored, and never shared. It works for confidential documents, contracts, and personal files. No watermarks are added to output files.
What types of PDFs convert best?
Text-based PDFs (PDFs created from Word, Google Docs, or other software that contain actual text data) convert with the best results. Simple single-column layouts produce the cleanest output. Complex multi-column layouts, tables, and graphics may not be perfectly preserved. Scanned PDFs (which are images of documents) cannot have their text extracted without OCR technology.
Can I convert specific pages of a PDF?
Yes! Select "Custom Range" in the Page Range option and enter your desired pages. You can specify individual pages (e.g. 1, 3, 5), ranges (e.g. 1-5), or a mix of both (e.g. 1-3, 7, 10-15). This is useful for extracting specific chapters or sections from large PDF documents.
What output formats are available?
You can download the converted content as a .docx (Microsoft Word) file, a plain .txt text file, or both simultaneously. The DOCX format preserves paragraph structure and heading detection. The TXT format is useful for pasting text into any application or editor.
Why does my converted Word file look different from the PDF?
PDF and Word are fundamentally different formats. PDF is a fixed-layout format designed for exact visual presentation. Word is a flow-based document format. Converting between them always involves some loss of complex formatting like multi-column layouts, precise text positioning, and embedded graphics. For text-heavy documents without complex layouts, the conversion is usually very accurate.
How large can the PDF file be?
This tool supports PDF files up to 50MB. For very large files, processing may take a little longer as it happens entirely in your browser. There is no page limit — you can convert PDFs with hundreds of pages.

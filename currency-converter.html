❓ Frequently Asked Questions – Currency Converter

How accurate are the exchange rates?
This converter fetches live exchange rates from the open.er-api.com free API, which updates rates daily from multiple financial data sources. The rates are indicative market rates — actual rates from banks and money exchange services may differ slightly due to fees, spreads and margins. For large financial transactions, always verify with your bank or forex provider.
How many currencies are supported?
This currency converter supports 170+ world currencies including all major currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CHF, CAD, AUD), regional currencies (PKR, INR, BDT, NPR), Middle Eastern currencies (AED, SAR, KWD, QAR), African currencies, and more. The full list includes currencies from virtually every country in the world.
What is the Multi-Convert mode?
Multi-Convert lets you convert a single amount from one currency into multiple currencies simultaneously. Enter your amount and select your source currency — the tool will instantly show the equivalent value in 20+ major world currencies. This is useful for travelers, businesses and investors who need to compare values across many currencies at once.
How does the Travel Budget calculator work?
In the Travel Budget mode, select your home currency and destination currency, then add your expected expenses as line items (accommodation, food, transport, activities etc.) in the destination currency. The tool converts each item and shows you the total cost in your home currency, helping you plan your travel budget accurately before your trip.
What happens if live rates are not available?
If the live rate API is unavailable (due to network issues or API limits), the converter automatically falls back to recent reference rates. The status badge at the top of the converter shows whether you are seeing live rates or fallback rates, so you always know the freshness of the data being used.

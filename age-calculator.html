❓ Frequently Asked Questions – Age Calculator

How do I calculate my exact age?
Enter your date of birth in the Age Calculator above and click "Calculate Age". The tool will instantly show your exact age in years, months, days, hours, minutes and even seconds. You can also choose a custom end date to calculate your age on any specific date.
How many days old am I?
Our Age Calculator shows your exact total days lived in the "Breakdown" tab after you calculate your age. For example, a 25-year-old person has lived approximately 9,125 days (25 × 365.25).
How does the Age Calculator determine my zodiac sign?
The zodiac sign is determined by your birth month and day. For example, if you were born between March 21 and April 19, you are an Aries. The calculator automatically detects and displays your Western zodiac sign along with your Chinese zodiac animal based on your birth year.
Can I calculate the age difference between two dates?
Yes! Select "Custom Date" in the "Calculate Age To" dropdown and enter any target date. The calculator will compute the exact age or difference between your birth date and the custom date you choose.
Is this Age Calculator free to use?
Absolutely! ToolNex's Age Calculator is 100% free with no sign-up, no account creation, and no hidden fees. It works directly in your browser and doesn't store any personal data.
How accurate is this Age Calculator?
The calculator is highly accurate and accounts for leap years and varying month lengths. It calculates the exact difference between your birth date and today (or any custom date) down to the second using standard calendar algorithms.
What is a birthstone and how is it determined?
A birthstone is a gemstone associated with the month of birth. For example, January's birthstone is Garnet, while December's is Turquoise. The calculator shows your birthstone based on your birth month automatically.

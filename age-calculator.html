❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How accurate is this age calculator?
Very accurate. It accounts for leap years and calculates to the exact second when using today's date.
Can I calculate age between two past dates?
Yes. Use the "Calculate Age At" field to set any target date — past or future — and the calculator will find the age at that exact point.
How is the zodiac sign determined?
Your Western/tropical zodiac sign is based on the Sun's position at your birth date. The calculator uses the standard date ranges for all 12 signs.
Are the heartbeats and breaths estimates?
Yes, these are approximate values based on average human rates: ~72 heartbeats/min and ~15 breaths/min. Individual results may vary.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

📅 Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Overview

ToolNex ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website at toolnex.site.

By using ToolNex, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide and improve our services:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, tools used, browser type, and device type — collected anonymously via Google Analytics.
  • Contact Information: If you email us, we receive your email address and message content.
  • No Personal Files: Most of our tools process files and data entirely in your browser. We do not store, upload, or access any files you use with our tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To understand how our tools are used and improve them
  • To respond to your support requests or feedback
  • To monitor site performance and fix issues

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics. This may use cookies to track page views and user behavior. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We may also show ads through Google AdSense, which uses cookies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing history.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any data we hold. Since most tools operate entirely in your browser, your files and personal data are never transmitted to our servers.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of ToolNex after changes means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@toolnex.site.

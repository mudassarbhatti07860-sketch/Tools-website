❓ Frequently Asked Questions – Online Notepad

Are my notes saved automatically?
Yes, notes are auto-saved to your browser's localStorage as you type. There is no server involved — your notes stay on your device and never leave your browser. You can also press Ctrl+S to save manually. The save indicator in the top bar shows "Saved ✓" when the note is up to date. Notes persist across browser sessions as long as you don't clear your browser data.
How many notes can I create?
You can create as many notes as your browser's localStorage allows (typically 5-10MB). Most users can store hundreds of notes. Each note has a title and body. You can search through all notes using the search box in the left panel. Notes are sorted by last modified date by default.
Can I download my notes?
Yes. Click the ⬇️ button in the top bar to download the current note as a .txt file. The filename uses the note title. For longer documents, you can also copy the entire note content using the 📋 button and paste it anywhere.
What does Find & Replace do?
Click 🔍 Find or press Ctrl+H to open the Find & Replace panel. Type the text you want to find in the first field — the tool highlights matching occurrences and shows the count. Enter replacement text in the second field and click "Replace All" to replace every occurrence at once. This is useful for correcting repeated mistakes or updating names throughout a document.
What is the Quick Checklist feature?
The Quick Checklist panel (☑️ List button) lets you create a temporary task list alongside your note. Add items, check them off, and when you're done, click "Insert into Note" to paste the checklist into your note body as checkbox symbols (☐ item / ☑ done). This is useful for todo lists, packing lists, or meeting action items.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are all tools really free?
Yes — every tool on ToolNex is 100% free. No hidden charges, no premium plans, no sign-up required. All tools are free forever.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. Most of our tools work entirely in your browser — your files and data never get uploaded to any server. Your privacy is our priority.
How long does it take to get a reply?
We typically reply within 24–48 hours on business days. For urgent issues, please mention it in the subject line of your email.
Can I suggest a new tool?
Yes! We love suggestions. Email us at support@toolnex.site with your idea and we'll consider adding it to ToolNex.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions – Lorem Ipsum Generator

What is Lorem Ipsum and why is it used?
Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text derived from Cicero's "de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" written in 45 BC. It has been used as standard dummy text in the typesetting and printing industry since the 1500s. Designers and developers use Lorem Ipsum because it looks like natural readable text in terms of letter distribution and word length, allowing them to focus on visual design and layout without being distracted by meaningful content.
What is the difference between Paragraphs, Words, Sentences and Bytes mode?
Paragraphs mode generates complete paragraphs of text — best for most design mockups. Words mode generates exactly the specified number of words — useful when you need to fill a text area of a specific size. Sentences mode generates the exact number of individual sentences — useful for testing sentence-level typography. Bytes mode generates text up to the specified byte size — useful for testing character limits in databases or APIs.
Can I get the Lorem Ipsum text in HTML format?
Yes! Enable the "Output as HTML" toggle before generating. This wraps each paragraph in <p> tags, making it ready to paste directly into your HTML files. You can also enable "Add headings" to insert <h2> tags before some paragraphs. After generating, switch between Preview view (rendered HTML) and Code view (raw HTML source) using the view toggle buttons.
What are the different text themes?
Besides the classic Latin Lorem Ipsum, this generator offers Business (professional business vocabulary), Tech Jargon (technology and software terminology), Nature (natural world vocabulary), Food (culinary terms), and Space (astronomy and space exploration terms). These themed variants are useful when you want placeholder text that looks more topically relevant to your project's subject matter, making client presentations more convincing.
Does the "Start with Lorem ipsum" option matter?
The "Start with Lorem ipsum…" option ensures the generated text begins with the famous opening phrase "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit…" This is the traditional and recognizable opening that most designers and clients expect. When disabled, the text starts with a randomly generated sentence from the selected word pool, which may be preferable for themed text like Business or Tech where the Latin opening would look out of place.

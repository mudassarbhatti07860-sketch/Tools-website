❓ FAQ
What YouTube URL formats are supported? ▾

All YouTube URL formats work: youtube.com/watch?v=ID, youtu.be/ID, youtube.com/embed/ID, youtube.com/shorts/ID and youtube.com/v/ID.

Why is MaxRes thumbnail not available for some videos? ▾

Not all YouTube videos have MaxRes (1280×720) thumbnails. Older videos or videos with low view counts may only have SD or HQ quality thumbnails available.

Is it legal to download YouTube thumbnails? ▾

YouTube thumbnails are publicly accessible images. However, they may be copyrighted by the video creator. Only use downloaded thumbnails for personal reference or with proper permission from the creator.

What is the highest quality thumbnail I can download? ▾

The highest quality is MaxRes at 1280×720 pixels (HD). When available, this provides the sharpest and largest thumbnail image for the video.

Can I download thumbnails from YouTube Shorts? ▾

Yes! YouTube Shorts have the same thumbnail system as regular videos. Paste the Shorts URL and all available thumbnails will be fetched just like any other video.
